Established 2024

Grown in the Tropics.
Roasted by the Tay.

Tay Tides began with a simple observation: Dundee's waterfront was undergoing a renaissance, but it needed a heart. We set out to create a space that honoured the city’s industrial "Jute, Jam, and Journalism" past while embracing its future as a global hub of design.

The Roast

We work exclusively with small-batch Scottish roasters who prioritise direct trade relationships with farmers in Ethiopia and Colombia.

The Community

More than a cafe, we are a gallery for local Dundee artists and a quiet corner for the city's creators and students.

The Future

By 2027, our goal is to become Dundee’s first carbon negative coffee space, using 100% compostable circular systems.

A Nod to the Silvery Tay

Our name is a tribute to the river that defines our city. Just as the tides bring change, we believe every cup of coffee is an opportunity to refresh and inspire. Our interiors feature reclaimed wood from old Scottish mills and stone that mirrors the V&A’s iconic facade.
